Description


The villa can accommodate up to 8 people, there are 4 double bedrooms with en suite bathrooms equipped with air conditioning, two bedrooms have a sea view and the other two have a sea view. garden. Precisely, a sea view room and a garden view room are accessible from the garden to provide privacy and independence. Around the villa there is a large patio with outdoor furniture for organizing outdoor lunches and dinners.
The rooms are large and have a private bathroom with shower, some have a sea view, and a patio with comfortable armchairs to enjoy the magical atmosphere of the Tuscan archipelago. They are also equipped with Wi-Fi, air conditioning, hairdryer, TV, fridge and coffee machine. They all benefit from a panoramic terrace overlooking the sea, ideal for breakfasts and aperitifs.
Some rooms have a sea view and others a garden view.
Swimming pool, The swimming pool is 30 meters from the villa and is located in the most panoramic point of the garden from where you can admire them magnificent sunsets over the sea, it is immersed in the greenery of olive trees and vegetation Mediterranean that guarantee privacy. Measure mt 12x4.50, accessed via the Roman staircase it is sanitized with salt, on one side there is an outdoor shower. The solarium area it is paved and furnished with sunbeds and umbrellas. The villa It has a barbecue area with an outdoor table, and there is also covered parking.
